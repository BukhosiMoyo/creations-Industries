## Anchor Text Rules

1. **Use descriptive anchor text.** Never use "click here", "learn more", or "read more" as standalone link text.
2. **Good examples:**
   - "View our bookkeeping services"
   - "Learn about CIPC annual return filing"
   - "Request a quote for payroll services"
3. **Bad examples:**
   - "Click here"
   - "Read more"
   - "See details"
4. **Consistency:** Use the full service name as written in the services list when linking to a service page.
