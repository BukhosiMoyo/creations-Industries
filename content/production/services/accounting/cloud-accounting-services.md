## 12. FAQs
**Is the cloud really safe for my financial data?**
Yes. Modern cloud accounting platforms use bank-level encryption (128-bit SSL or higher) and have multiple redundant backups. It is far more secure than having your data on a single laptop that could be lost or stolen.

**Do I lose my data if I cancel the service?**
No. You own your data. If you decide to move, you can export your records or take your cloud subscription with you to a new practitioner.

**What if I don't have a reliable internet connection?**
Cloud accounting platforms are designed to be light. Even a basic mobile data connection is usually enough to record transactions or view reports.

**Can you use my existing Xero or Sage account?**
Yes. If you already have a subscription, you simply invite us as your "Advisor" and we start working on your live data immediately.

**Does this mean I don't need to see you in person?**
Cloud accounting allows for a 100% remote relationship, but we are always available for in-person or virtual meetings to discuss your strategic direction.
