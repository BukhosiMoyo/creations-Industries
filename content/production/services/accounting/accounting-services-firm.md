## 12. FAQs
**What is the difference between a bookkeeper and an accounting firm?**
A bookkeeper records data. An accounting firm provides high-level analysis, strategic tax planning, and the legal "sign-off" on financial statements that only registered practitioners can provide.

**Do I get to talk to a real person at the firm?**
Absolutely. While we use technology for efficiency, you will have a dedicated accountant at the firm who understands your business and is available for consultations.

**Is a firm more expensive than a freelancer?**
Not necessarily. Because we are highly efficient and use automated systems, our fees are often very competitive compared to the "hidden costs" of errors or missed deadlines common with less structured services.

**Are you registered with SARS as a tax practitioner?**
Yes. Our firm and its practitioners are fully registered with SARS and the relevant recognized controlling bodies (RCBs).

**Can you help us if our business is in a crisis?**
Yes. Professional firms often specialize in "restructuring" and "turnaround" accounting, helping businesses get back on track with clean records and solid financial advice.
