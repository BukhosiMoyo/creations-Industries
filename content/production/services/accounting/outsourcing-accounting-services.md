## 12. FAQs
**Is it safe to give an external firm access to my bank?**
We use "view-only" access for reconciling. For payments, we use secure authorization workflows where you remain the final approver on all transactions. Your security is our highest priority.

**How is this different from a normal accountant?**
A typical accountant might only see you once a year. Outsourcing means we act as your **internal** finance department, but based externally. We are involved in your business every week.

**Do I lose control of my data?**
Quite the opposite. With cloud accounting, you have *more* control. You can see your dashboard and real-time profit figures from your phone at any time.

**Can you scale down if my business has a slow month?**
Yes. Our service models are designed to be flexible. If your transaction volume drops significantly, our fees can adjust accordingly.

**What about my current bookkeeper?**
Many of our clients have internal staff for daily admin and outsource only the high-level accounting, tax, and payroll to us. We can build a hybrid model that works for you.
