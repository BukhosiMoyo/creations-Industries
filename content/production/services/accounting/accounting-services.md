## 2. WHAT ARE ACCOUNTING SERVICES?
Accounting services provide the essential framework for tracking, analyzing, and reporting a company's financial health. Beyond simple record-keeping, professional accounting ensures that every transaction is correctly classified, reconciled, and presented in formal financial statements that meet South African regulatory standards.

For a growing business, accounting is the bridge between daily bookkeeping and strategic financial planning, providing the data needed for tax filing, funding applications, and performance reviews.

---

## 3. WHY ACCOUNTING SERVICES MATTER (WITH CONTEXT)
Many South African SMEs operate with "financial blind spots" that only become apparent during tax season or when applying for finance. Typically, businesses without professional accounting support face risks such as:

- **Surprise Tax Liabilities:** Not accounting for VAT or provisional tax throughout the year.
- **Funding Rejections:** Lacking the compliant Annual Financial Statements (AFS) required by South African banks and lenders.
- **Cash Flow Bottlenecks:** Missing the data needed to spot trends before they become crises.
- **Compliance Gaps:** Falling behind on CIPC annual returns or SARS eFiling requirements.

On average, businesses with monthly accounting oversight are significantly better prepared for audits and tender opportunities compared to those that only review their books once a year.

## 12. FAQs
**How much do your accounting services cost?**
Pricing is based on transaction volume and the complexity of your reporting requirements. We offer packages tailored for SMEs to ensure you only pay for the oversight you actually need.

**Can you catch up on multiple years of outstanding records?**
Yes. We specialize in reconstruction and cleanup for businesses that have fallen behind. We will provide a specific catch-up quote after an initial assessment.

**Do I need to switch my software to work with you?**
Not necessarily. We work with Xero, Sage, and QuickBooks. If you are using an outdated or manual system, we can help you migrate to a more efficient cloud solution.

**How is this different from bookkeeping?**
Bookkeeping is the daily recording of transactions. Accounting is the analysis, professional adjustment, and formal reporting of that data to ensure it is compliant and strategically useful.

**Will this help me get a bank loan or a tender?**
Absolutely. Most South African banks and government tenders require at least two years of professionally prepared Annual Financial Statements. Our service ensures yours are ready and compliant.
