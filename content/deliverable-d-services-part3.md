### Meta

- **Description:** Tender preparation services for SA businesses. CSD registration, B-BBEE verification, tax clearance, and compliance document preparation. (140 chars)

### Service Intro (155 words)
Winning a government or corporate tender in South Africa starts long before the bid is submitted. Every tender requires a package of compliance documents: a valid tax clearance certificate, CSD (Central Supplier Database) registration, B-BBEE certificate or affidavit, proof of company registration, and often audited or reviewed financial statements. Missing or outdated documents mean disqualification — regardless of how competitive your bid is. Our tender readiness support ensures your compliance documentation is current, accurate, and in order before you submit. We help with CSD registration and profile management, tax clearance applications through SARS, B-BBEE affidavits and verification referrals, company documentation from CIPC, and preparation of financial statement packs. We do not write the bid itself, but we make sure the compliance side of your tender submission is airtight. If you are chasing contracts, we help you qualify.

### Deliverables
- CSD registration and profile management
- Tax clearance certificate applications
- B-BBEE affidavit preparation (EME/QSE)
- CIPC compliance documentation assembly
- Company registration and good standing confirmations
- Financial statement preparation for tender packs
- Document checklist and gap analysis
- Renewal tracking for time-sensitive documents

### Process Steps
1. **Requirements review** — We identify the compliance documents required for your target tenders.
2. **Gap analysis** — We check which documents you have, which are expired, and which need preparation.
3. **Document preparation** — We prepare, apply for, or renew all required compliance items.
4. **Pack assembly** — We compile a complete, organised tender compliance pack ready for submission.
5. **Ongoing maintenance** — We track document expiry dates and notify you when renewals are due.

### FAQs

**Q1: What is the CSD?**
A: The Central Supplier Database (CSD) is the South African government's supplier registration portal. Registration is mandatory for any business that wants to supply goods or services to a government entity.

**Q2: Do you write the actual tender bid?**
A: No. Our service covers the compliance and financial documentation required to qualify. For bid writing, we can refer you to a specialist.

**Q3: How long does it take to get a tax clearance certificate?**
A: If all your SARS returns are up to date and there are no outstanding debts, the certificate is typically issued within a few business days. If there are outstanding issues, it will take longer and we can help resolve them.

**Q4: What is a B-BBEE affidavit?**
A: Businesses with turnover under R10 million (EMEs) or under R50 million (QSEs, in certain cases) can use a sworn affidavit to confirm their B-BBEE level. This is a simpler alternative to a full verification process.

**Q5: Can you help if my CSD profile has errors?**
A: Yes. We review and correct CSD profile information, update contact and banking details, and resolve common verification issues.

**Q6: What documents do government tenders typically require?**
A: Most require: a valid tax clearance, CSD registration, B-BBEE certificate or affidavit, CIPC registration certificate, proof of banking, company directors information, and recent financial statements.

**Q7: Do these documents expire?**
A: Yes. Tax clearance certificates, B-BBEE certificates, and CSD registrations all have validity periods. We track these and prompt you before they lapse.
